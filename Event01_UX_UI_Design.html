Automotive Console UI Design EV01

The Brief


For this brief, we were asked to either choose an existing car model or create our own and design the UI UX for the vehicles screens.

The Solution


I knew I wanted to create my own vehicle but not only did I want a car I wanted to design something high-tech and straight out of a sci-fi film. I opted for a spaceship that also could transform into a car. For the screens I wanted to make sure I added all the things a car and a spaceship would need. I went with a neon purple colour palette with a lot of glowing to compliment the sci-fi look I wanted to achieve.
